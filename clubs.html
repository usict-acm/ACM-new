ACM CLUBS

Your chase of excellence starts from here. ACM Clubs are a place to interact with people of similar skills. Come and join ACM Clubs to ensure that no technology is alien for you.

Appetite

The ACM Appetite Club is responsible for managing and organizing the application development activities and events organized under ACM USICT. The purpose behind this club is to promote learning and the culture of development in our college with regular doubt solving sessions.

This club is to promote a community of like-minded people who believe in collaborative application development and helping each other out whenever needed. Fun activities such as quiz, challenges, etc. are organized by the club under ACM throughout the year.

Webify

The ACM Webify Club is responsible for managing and organizing the web development activities and events organized under ACM USICT. The purpose behind this club is to promote learning and the culture of development in our college. It's a community of like-minded people who believe in collaborative web development and helping each other out whenever needed.

Fun activities such as quizzes, hackathons, etc. are organized by the club under ACM throughout the year where students can show off their skills and win swags.

Cyber Cell

The ACM-Cyber Cell Club, a community established by the very students of ACM USICT, is an initiative to raise awareness and encourage discussions regarding new Technologies, their drawbacks, exploitation and solution for correct use. This Cell is an initiative by ACM to raise awareness and encourage discussions with respect to Cyber laws, Cybercrimes and Cybersecurity.

With the technology, the club also promotes the enthusiastic learning of ethical hacking concepts in various domains such as Web Security, Network Security, and many more.

RobIOTics

RobIOTics is the Electronic Club of ACM USICT. It covers 3 major technical fields which are Robotics, the Internet of Things, and Robotic Process Automation. The goal of this club is to share knowledge, resources, and opportunities related to the specified fields with each other and build the electronic community of our college extensively.

This year we have planned many events like workshops and hands-on sessions. So we welcome all the tech-enthusiasts to join this adventurous journey of ACM RobIOTics.

Entrepreneurs' Arena

ACM Entrepreneurs' Arena Club under ACM USICT aims at developing the skills that bring about the change, generally entailing risk beyond what is normally encountered in starting a business, which may include other values than simply economic ones. The purpose of this club is to create a platform to bring individuals, who desire to establish, administer and succeed in a startup venture along with risk entitled to it, to make profits and contribute to society, together.

Through unique and distinctive ideas, this club helps in cultivating the quality of ingenuity in students.

Research Cell

Research Cell is not just a domain for highly qualified professionals but is a platform for anyone who has the curiosity to know and the ability to think out of the box. ACM USICT Research Cell aims towards flourishing the research culture of GGSIPU.

Our goal is to guide the students of USICT towards excellence in their domain of interest and to encourage them to conduct research and look for new possibilities in that domain, eventually improving India's contribution towards the advancement of humanity.

Innovate AI

The objective of Innovate AI club is to create awareness and equip the students with the ability as well as skills to analyze and understand the technology in the field of Artificial Intelligence and its possibilities. We aim to achieve this by organizing interesting and exciting events for all members with any type of background.

Together with different clubs under ACM, we work towards galvanizing a community that encourages collective learning and synergistic growth.

ICPC Club

The ICPC Club, USICT focuses on spreading the culture of Competitive Programming at different levels through the guidance of world achievers, alumni, and teacher's experience and most importantly hard work of every community member. Club's main motive is to develop global level programmers who can compete at every stage worldwide.

No doubt India has a great reputation when it comes to programmers but still, there is a scope of improvement and we want to fill that gap by combining the efforts of teachers, mentors, and the entire community.
